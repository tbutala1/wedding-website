Local Transportation

We will provide a shuttle service between the recommended hotels and wedding venues.

For those arriving by air, the nearest airport is Anytown International Airport (ANT), about 15 miles from the city center.

Rental cars, taxis, and rideshare services are also available in the area.

Things to Do in Anytown

If you're extending your stay, here are some local attractions:

  • Anytown Beach - Beautiful sandy beaches with water activities
  • Anytown Historical Museum - Learn about the city's rich history
  • Vineyard Tours - Several award-winning wineries within 10 miles
  • Hiking Trails - Mountain views and nature preserves
  • Downtown Shopping - Boutiques, galleries, and restaurants

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the dress code? +

The dress code for our wedding is semi-formal/cocktail attire. Men are encouraged to wear suits or slacks with a dress shirt and tie. Women may wear cocktail dresses or dressy separates.

Are children welcome at the wedding? +

While we love your little ones, we've decided to make our wedding an adults-only celebration. We hope this allows all of our guests to relax and enjoy the evening. We appreciate your understanding!

Will there be parking at the venues? +

Yes, complimentary parking is available at both the ceremony and reception venues. Valet service will also be available at the reception.

Can I bring a plus one? +

Due to venue constraints, we can only accommodate those guests explicitly named on your invitation. Thank you for your understanding.

What if I have dietary restrictions? +

We're happy to accommodate dietary restrictions. Please note any special requirements in your RSVP response.

Will the wedding be indoor or outdoor? +

The ceremony will be held indoors at the church, while the reception will take place in the Grand Ballroom with access to an outdoor terrace. In case of inclement weather, all activities will be moved indoors.
